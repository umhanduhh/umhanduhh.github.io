Welcome

Welcome! We're glad to have you.
Amateur Chefs Arcade is a passion project combining its creator's love for cooking and nutrition, and curiosity about coding and web development.

Here you'll find recipes, games, and blogs to help you have more fun eating foods that bring you joy and energy.
You won't find any weight loss tips, shame, or prescriptive rules to follow.

We're not registered dietitians, and believe that registered dietitians are the best people to give dietary advice and guidance to individuals.
We do love food and fueling our bodies, and want to share that love here.
If you'd like to connect with a registered dietitian, let us know, we'd love to introduce you to some great ones!

Thanks to chatGPT and my husband for all the help building this, and thanks to you for being here!
