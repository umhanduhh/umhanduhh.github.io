About

Amateur Chefs Arcade is a passion project combining it's creators love for cooking and nutrition, and curiousity about coding and web development. Here you'll find recipes, games, and blogs to help you have more fun eating foods that bring you joy and energy. You wont find any weight loss tips, shame, or prescriptive rules to follow.
